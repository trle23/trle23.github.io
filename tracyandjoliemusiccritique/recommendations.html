Beyonce

Beyonce Giselle Knowles-Carter was born on September 4, 1981 in Houston,
Texas. She has over 100 million records sold as a solo artist and 60 million
in her band, Destiny's Child. Beyonce is one of the best-selling music
artists of all time. She has described herself as a modern-day feminist, always
empowering women through her music. Her genre focuses on mostly pop
and hip-hop. She is a great artist to listen to when you just want to feel
confident and empowered.
